Road Edge Retaining Wall Catalog

A specialized Civil 3D subassembly designed for road edge retaining wall modeling. This catalog provides a parameter-driven solution for wall cases 2-12, including integrated earthwork and PCC foundation logic.

Key Features

  • Wall Cases 2-12: Pre-configured dimensions based on standard engineering tables.
  • Dynamic Overrides: Full control over specific dimensions when standards don't apply.
  • Corridor Automation: Native Civil 3D integration with proper point, link, and shape coding.
  • Integrated Surfaces: Companion command RW_CREATE_SURFACES for rapid surface generation.
  • Side Mirroring: Single subassembly handles both Left and Right side placement automatically.
